About me

My name is Ryan Harrold, and I am a 25 year old Software Engineer, based in Brisbane, Australia. I have been trained in both programming and game design, having completed a Bachelor of games and interactive environments in 2021. I currently work as a software engineer, working on a graphics engine used in construction software.

I have a great passion for programming, rivaled only by my love of games. I am comfortable in a range of Languages and technologies, including C++, C#, and OpenGL. I am also confident that I can learn new technologies as required. I first started learning how to program when I was 13 years old, seeking to understand how the games that I love were created.

I've come a long way since then, and look forward to learning more and continue honing my skills.
