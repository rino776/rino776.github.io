Quantum Pong

This game was developed as part of a team for IGB200. It is a fun re-imagining of the classic game 'pong' but with quantum machanic concepts involved. it is a local multiplayer game designed for 2 people.

In this prject, my main role was a programmer, but I also helped with the design from conception right to the final product. Unfortuantly, the final build never got uploaded to source control, and is now lost to time.
